FAQ

What is Green Division?

Programmers of DIU ACM are divided into three divisions on the basis of their solves- Green, Blue & Red. Usually, we suggest beginners to solve problems from URI Online Judge as there are plenty of easy problems there, to solve from and to take baby steps towards growth. And this category of programmers are defined as the Green Division. And to join Blue Division every programmer has to go through Green Division. And to be in the Red Division they have to go through Blue Division.



Why should I join Green Division?

As mentioned earlier to join Blue Division one has to go through Green Division. And DIU ACM arranges training classes for Blue Division. Also to track the programmers DIU ACM uses these Divisions. And if someone is not a part of any Division and DIU ACM becomes unable to track them then they might miss out on an opportunity to go to a national programming contest as well. And most importantly if someone is solving with a guideline will help to progress more than solving randomly. That is where this website comes in handy.



Why this website has a list of problems whereas previously one could solve any problem from URI?

URI has a lot of problems and it's only increasing with quite a speed. So there are some very similar problems. So solving those problems only increases the number of solves and the practice doesn't stays very efficient anymore. So, here the senior programmers of DIU has selected a variety of problems in different categories. So that, the practice stays efficient.



How do I become eligible for Green Division?

Create an account on URI Online Judge then using that account register on this wensite and you are good to go. Keep solving problems from URI.



What if I get stuck while solving the problems?

If you get stuck and need help programmers of our ACM community are always eager to help. And best way to get help even if you don't any senior programmers is to join our ACM community DIU ACM Solver Forum. Link: https://www.facebook.com/groups/cppsdiu. Here you can post your problems and the seniors or your fellow programmers will be more than happy to help. You can ask for suggestions, advise regarding practice and problem solving there as well.



What is after Green Divison?

As mentioned in the answers for the previous questions after one has solved a good amount of problem or more specifically at least 60% problems from each category and has solved 160+ problem in total can advance to Blue Division. For that, eligible programmers will find an "Apply for Blue" button on the Eligibility page. Don't worry the "Apply for Blue" button will automatically become clickable once a programmer has fulfilled the criteria mentioned above.



How does this website work?

Introduction to the Green Division Website-
Standings: On this page, There will be a list of registered Green Division programmers. Their Name, URI profile Link, and total solve count. By clicking on the name we can go to their Green division profile and see their information and the problems they have solved.
About: This page holds the information about the website, Green Division, Problem Contributors and developers.

To access the other pages you have log in so...

Register: Problem solvers can register filling the information in this page after that they will have to confirm/verify the email as well before logging in for the first time.
Log In: Green Division programmers can log in using the email and password they registered with.
Profile: A Green Division programmer can see and edit their information from this page. Also can see which problems they have solved already from the list.
Eligibility: Here a Green Division programmer can see what percantage of problems they have solved from which category. So that, they can so which category is lacking solves for applying for blue. Also they will find the option to apply for blue from this page.
